# Ophthalmology Service Line Operations Simulation

## Purpose
This repository contains a high-fidelity simulated digital workspace designed to evaluate the reasoning, data synthesis, and operational judgement of frontier AI agents in complex healthcare environments.

It was built by an experienced NHS Ophthalmic Technician to mirror the messy, lived-in reality of hospital operations—complete with conflicting priorities, missing data, and cross-departmental friction.

## Contents
The workspace is structured into a typical Google Drive hierarchy, containing 40 interrelated documents:
- **01_Performance_Reporting**: Dashboards and theatre utilization trackers.
- **02_Quality_and_Safety**: Incident reports, clinical audits, and risk registers.
- **03_Finance_and_Budget**: Variance reports and business cases.
- **04_Workforce_and_Staffing**: Nursing rosters and agency spend data.
- **05_Service_Improvement_and_Pathways**: Clinical SOPs and escalation protocols.
- **06_Clinical_System_Exports**: Raw CSV/Excel data mimicking PAS and Medisoft outputs.
- **07_Correspondence_and_Emails**: Simulated email threads containing operational context and professional friction.

## Evaluation Tasks
The `TASK_SPECIFICATIONS.md` file contains 5 multi-step challenges designed to test AI agents. These tasks require cross-referencing between 3 to 6 different files and navigating deliberate data anomalies.

## Getting Started
Open `index.html` in your browser to view the complete project portfolio, workspace architecture, and task rubrics.
